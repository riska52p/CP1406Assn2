Result of Usability & Accessibility Testing

N = 5
Ø = 3/3 points

Issues:

1 out of 5 persons claimed the website to be “too complicated”
Possible explanation: She was not able to find the order form straight away

Recommendations:

1 out of 5 persons found typography mistakes
Note: corrected immediately afterwards

1 out of 5 persons complained about the length of the product description
Note: subjective taste, nonetheless we consider this critic

1 out of 5 persons recommends our “search function”
Note: Search bar is one of the most difficult parts of the page and we tried our best

PMI

Plus:

  • Each team member had her individual skill referring to web design and therefore work was done efficiently and very fast
  • Interest in topic was generally high and team members were very committed in learning more in spare time

Minus:

  • Some expectations were too high for level 1 students e.g. JavaScript (completely self-taught), searchbar function. Therefore very time consuming for programmers in group (other assignments were also important and it was stressful)
  • GitHub was more an obstacle than a help as we experienced a lot of synching problems and conflicts
