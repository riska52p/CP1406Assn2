Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

It is likely that your question has been asked before! Check our FAQ for your inquiery – if you cannot find the topic you’re interested in. Please contact us!

  1. Where is Diazepam?

    Diazepam is located in Singapore and has 5 outlets.

  2. Do you offer other products than ice-cream?

    We are an exclusive seller of ice-cream related deserts.

    We keep on creating specialities for you which is promoted online and in our stores.

    Stay updated and you might find what you are looking for.

  3. I want to work for you, where can I apply to?

    We are working on a career site. Keep in mind that we are a smaller business which wants to keep the family atmosphere in the cafes and between workers.

    Do not hesitate to talk to our staff members and check posters and flyers in our cafes which offer positions.

    You can also contact us to have the information about any vacancies.

  4. I am not satisfied with your products. Can I get a refund?

    We try our best to satisfy you with our delicious product but we know taste differs.

    Unfortunately, it is not possible to refund products once purchased and consumed.

    Please refer to our Terms and Conditions Refund Policy

  5. Can I bring my pets into cafes?

    Unfortunately, due to hygienic standards (Singaporean Law) it is not allowed to bring any pets to our cafes.
