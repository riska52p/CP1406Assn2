About Us

Do you love ice cream?

We live and breathe them.

Desert time is the best time of the day – so why not enjoying it as often as you want to?

Diazepam café will enchant you with unique ice cream creations & hand-crafted deserts all made with natural flavors and no artificial additives, because we believe that you deserve the best taste and culinary pleasure every day.

  • Try our latest mouthwatering ice-cream creations on a rainy afternoon
  • Take a sip from our delicious ice-cream floats before work
  • Dream away on our soft tasteful cakes with your best friends

All in a cozy vintage atmosphere.

You will be overwhelmed by the wide variety of our flavors and lovely products but no worries – Our service team loves to recommend the desert best suiting for you.
